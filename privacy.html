Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

DC Kids Brand ("we", "us") operates this online store for children's fashion in Ghana. This policy explains what information we collect when you use it, why, and the choices you have. We keep it short because we collect very little.

What we collect

  • When you place an order: your name, phone number, delivery area, order items, and any note you add. We need these to prepare your order and reach you on WhatsApp to confirm and arrange delivery and payment.
  • When you write a review: the name you choose to display and your review text.
  • On your device only: your cart, wishlist, and display preferences are stored in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device unless you place an order.
  • Staff accounts (admin): staff name and email, used solely to sign in to the dashboard.

We do not collect card details or process online payments — payment is arranged directly with us via WhatsApp or on delivery. We do not use advertising trackers or analytics cookies.

How we use it

  • To fulfil and deliver your order and contact you about it.
  • To keep simple sales records so we can run the business (best-sellers, stock).
  • To display reviews you chose to publish.

Who we share it with

Nobody, except the services that make the store work: WhatsApp (order conversations you start), Telegram (order alerts to the shop owner), and — for staff sign-in only — Resend (sign-in code emails) and Google (optional "Continue with Google"). We never sell your information.

How long we keep it

Order records are kept for as long as we need them for business and legal record-keeping. You can ask us to delete your personal details at any time.

Your choices

  • Message us on WhatsApp at +233 54 919 3805 or email info@dckidsbrand.com to see, correct, or delete the details we hold about you.
  • Clear your browser storage to remove your local cart and wishlist.

Security

Our systems use encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access to order information is limited to authorised staff who sign in with one-time codes. No system is perfectly secure, but we keep the amount of personal data we hold deliberately small.

Children

We sell children's clothing to parents and guardians. The store is not intended to be used by children themselves, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will appear here with an updated date.
