These terms apply when you shop with DC Kids Brand ("we", "us"), a children's fashion retailer operating in Ghana. Placing an order means you accept them.

1. How ordering works

  • You place an order on this site with your name, phone number, and delivery area. The order is a request — it becomes final only when we confirm it with you on WhatsApp.
  • Prices are in Ghana cedis (GH₵). Totals are calculated by our system at the moment you order; delivery charges are agreed on WhatsApp based on your location.
  • Payment is arranged directly with us (mobile money or cash on delivery, as agreed). We never ask for card details on this website.

2. Wholesale orders

Wholesale prices apply per piece with a discount, subject to a minimum order quantity (MOQ) per item shown at the time of ordering. Wholesale orders below the MOQ cannot be placed.

3. Pre-orders (China collection)

Pre-order items are sourced on request and require a deposit to confirm, which we arrange on WhatsApp. Delivery timelines for pre-orders are estimates and can shift with shipping and customs; we keep you updated.

4. Availability and pricing errors

All items are subject to availability. If an item in your order is out of stock, or a listing contained an obvious pricing error, we will tell you before confirming and you may adjust or cancel that item at no cost.

5. Delivery

We deliver nationwide in Ghana through third-party riders and transport services. Risk in the goods passes to you on delivery. Please check your items with the rider where possible.

6. Exchanges and returns

  • Tell us on WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery about any defect, damage, or wrong item, and we will exchange it or refund what you paid for that item.
  • For hygiene reasons we cannot accept returns of worn or washed items, or items without their original packaging, unless they were defective.

7. Reviews

Reviews you publish must be honest and lawful. We may remove reviews that are abusive, fake, or unrelated to the product.

8. Our liability

Nothing in these terms removes rights you have under Ghanaian consumer protection law. Beyond what the law requires, our responsibility for any order is limited to the amount you paid for it.
